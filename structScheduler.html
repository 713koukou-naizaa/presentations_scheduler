◆ TrySchedulingResultOptions

enum Scheduler::TrySchedulingResultOptions

The resulting schedule as a vector of scheduled presentations.

Result options for a scheduling attempt SCHEDULED if the student was successfully scheduled SAME_DAY if the student couldn't be scheduled but could be retried later the same day NEXT_WEEK if the student couldn't be scheduled and should be retried next week NOT_POSSIBLE if the student couldn't be scheduled at all
